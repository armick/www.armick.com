The RABIT

Efficient. Accurate.

RABIT

Radial Axial Bearing Inspection Tool

The RABIT is a patented inspection tool that measures spherical bearing wear during quality inspections and overhauls. It measures axial and radial bearing play with accuracy up to 1/1000 of an inch. It can be deployed for on-wing inspections or used on the bench during repair and overhaul. Other uses include initial quality inspection and parts validation. Save costly flight test time and ensure the safety and airworthiness of your aircraft with the RABIT.

Features

  • Accurate to 1/1000 of an inch
  • Measures both axial and radial play
  • Three gauges allow the user to isolate the axial and radial measurements on multiple size bearings in multiple link configurations (magnetic or non-magnetic)
  • On-wing inspection
  • Save costly flight test time
  • Cost effective
  • Lightweight and nimble
  • Includes durable carrying case
