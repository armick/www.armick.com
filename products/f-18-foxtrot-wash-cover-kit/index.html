F-18 Foxtrot Wash Cover Set

F18-FOXTROT-WCS7

The Armick F-18 Aircraft Wash Cover Set features 7 covers that protect sensitive components when performing an aircraft wash on the F-18 airframe. Each cover is bright yellow. The reusable covers will help prevent FOD as they are custom fit to each component.  The covers included are a Cockpit Canopy Cover, two SDCC Covers, two Front Wheel Covers, and two Rear Wheel Covers. 

  • Resuable
  • Highly visible when installed
  • Reduces risk of FOD
  • Protects from water intrusion
