The Armick 90W Battery Powered Mobile Light Station provides a quiet, convenient, and ecologically responsible alternative to diesel fueled generator light sets. Built with versatility in mind, the light station features a telescoping mast and a 360° articulating light array.  The system's next-generation LEDs emit up to 9,9000 lumens.  Powered by a rechargeable battery, the system offers hours of run time.  In a self-contained case with the mast mounted on the exterior, the unit is easily trasportable while deployed.

  • Ideal for emergency warehouse lighting, temporary indoor/outdoor task lighting, nighttime event lighting, or etc.
  • Battery operated with 12 volts, 110-amp hour AGM battery
  • Battery charge indicator and protection circuit
  • Single person setup/breakdown time of 2.5 minutes
  • Red perimeter lights and white accessory lights for safety and easy of setup
  • Up to 10 hours of run time
  • 9,900 lumes of light
  • 60° light pattern
  • Deisnged for use on the flight line
  • Light mast height ranges from 5-15.5 ft. (1.5-4.7 m)
  • Six LED light heads can be independently rotated
  • 360° articulating light array
  • All components attach to wheeled battery box for easy, 1-person transport
Electrical requirements 120 VAC at 60 Hz, 2 amps (GFCI protected outlet) for battery charger only
Operating voltage 12 VDCa fda
Battery charge time (from full depletion) 12 hours
Battery run time (from full charge) 10 hours at full power, 20 hours at half power
Light mast height range 5-15.5 ft. (1.5-4.7 m)
Light type LED lights
