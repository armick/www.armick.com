The 50 gallon soap foamer unit operates on compressed air and requires only ready-to-use solution to create thick, long-lasting foam.

  • Foam eliminates the need for high pressure cleaning
  • Foam reduces the amount of labor needed to clean the aircraft
  • Foam provides maximum contact time for chemical effectiveness
  • Foam offers savings in chemical and water usage
  • 60 ft. industrial hose for maximum soap foam volume and distance
  • Turf tread wheels for increased durability
